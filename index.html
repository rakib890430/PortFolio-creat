2020- Junior | Web Developer jurney

My start was not very easy and it was not very difficult. The first is that you need
to have a clear and ambitious reason why you want to be a web developer.
This can be quite subjective, so not everyone has to agree with my idea, but make
sure your reasons are enough to keep you going. But some of the reasons I'm so
discouraged are "I want to be a developer to make money" or "I want to be a developer
to create applications." Aren't all developers working for this reason? Well, most successful
developers have all of these factors not just as their main focus, but as the output of what they have done. After thinking a lot, I somehow decided that I would be a web developer and I am slowly going down that path. I wish blessings to all.
